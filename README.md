**1.Internship Updates and Full Time Updates Groups**<br>
i).  The Internship/Full Time Updates group includes community groups that are **titled** with **sequence numbers** (e.g., Internship Updates 1, 2, and 3, Full Time Updates 1,2 and 3).<br>
ii).  The Internship/Full Time Updates group also includes **sub-groups** that are titled with sequence letters (e.g., Internship Updates 1-A, 1-B, and 1-C).<br>
iii).  To avoid **duplication** and **for your convenience**, you should only join **one sequence community and subgroups** and **leave all other groups**.For example, you can join the Internship Updates 1 community and Internship Updates 1-A subgroup under the Internship Updates 1 community .<br><br>
**2.Accommodation/Sublease Post Groups**<br>
i). You can join and post on the group/subgroup of **state** in which you are **staying**<br>
ii). You can join and post on the group/subgroup of **state**in which you are leaving<br>
iii). You can join and post on the group/subgroup of **state** in which you are joining
